Services

Kant Medical Services specializes in (Hospital Supply , Chemist retail supply & Medicine home delivery supply)

Hospital Supply

Hospital supply chain management is the regulation of the flow of medicines from manufacturer to hospitals . Hospital supply chain management involves obtaining resources , managing supplies and delivering goods and services hospitals.

Chemist Retail Supply

The Chemist Supply Chain is the means through which prescription medicines are manufactured and delivered to patients .We fill medication orders placed by doctors for customers who come into their pharmacy or chemish shop

Medicine Home Delivery Supply

We ensuring that the delivery of medicines to patients is carried out in a safe, oraganisd and secure manner .
